What is ZcashPunk?

ZcashPunk is an NFT project based on the Zcash blockchain, featuring 10,000 unique pixel-style characters. Each ZcashPunk has a unique combination of attributes, including different appearances, accessories, and rarity levels.

We are committed to combining classic pixel art with Zcash's privacy protection technology, bringing a new experience to the world of digital collectibles.
